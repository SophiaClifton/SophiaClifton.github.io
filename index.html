Sophia's Games

The Walk is a platformer game created for the 2 week 2023 New Year Game Jam hosted by Gx Games following the theme "Procedurally Generated".

The Walk is an art driven game that follows the journey of a fox through procedurally generated terrain. The fox walks, runs and jumps towards your cursor as you guide it along. Or, if you'd like to simply enjoy the ambient music and sounds of the forest, feel free to keep it playing as the fox takes a nap.

Published 19 January, 2023

With You Again is a story and art driven multiplayer platformer conceptualized and created for the 48hrs 2023 Poly Games/Ubisoft Gamejam.

Following the theme " Last Chance", this game offers one last chance to a couple in the afterlife, reimagined as spirit animals, to reignite their love and make it back to life. Together, solve puzzles and overcome multiple levels using communication and teamwork skills.

Published 17 September, 2023

Amouti's Journey was created for the 48hrs CGD 2023 Game Jam. It is a single-player puzzle solving platformer.

Playing on the theme "The Space Between", play as Amouti, an alien trapped in the space between worlds trying to return to his family behind a locked portal. Explore alien worlds, solve puzzles and defeat enemies to collect the keys to unlock the portal and help Amouti return home.

Published 5 November, 2023

Chicken Run is a single-player 3D runner game created by me for the Concordia Intro to Game Development class.

Chicken Run is a third person running game which increases in difficulty over time. Choose from three game modes each varying in difficulty and run for as long as you can collecting coins and abilties. Don't get fried in fire or skewered in spikes, and beat age by gaining time boosts while running against a countdown.

Published 29 November, 2023
