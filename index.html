Sophia's Games

The Walk is a platformer game created for the 2023 Gx Games Gamejam following the theme "Procedurally Generated".

The Walk is an art driven game that follows the journey of a fox through procedurally generated terrain. The fox walks, runs and jumps towards your cursor as you guide it along. Or, if you'd like to simply enjoy the ambient music and sounds of the forest, feel free to keep it playing as the fox takes a nap.

With You Again is a story and art driven platformer conceptualized and created for the 2023 Poly Games/Ubisoft Gamejam.

Following the theme " Last Chance", this game offers once last chance to a couple in the afterlife, reimagined as spirit animals, to reignite their love and make it back to life. Together they must overcome multiple challenges and levels, strengthing their communication and teamwork skills, and ultimately bringing them back together.
